My Services

I have experience with couples and individuals going through life and communication issues. I have worked with many individuals who have lost a spouse or partner through death or divorce and need help and support coping.

I also work with adults coping with career transitions or anger management issues.

One of my strengths is that I provide a good listening ear, give suggestions if the client is open to it and work to help the clients reach their potential.

My ideal client is one committed to taking positive steps to reach treatment goals and following up with appointments. However, a path to a better life does not end outside of a session. The ideal client also follows up outside of sessions with steps for self improvement.

Volunteer

I believe some of my best experience comes from volunteerism in the community.

Mission Statement

My practice focuses on three areas. I work with individuals and couples faced with relationship issues. I assist individuals and couples coping with grief and loss and I assist adults in life transitions.


My goal is to help my clients restore hope. Concurrently, I help my clients reduce stress and reframe their negative feelings. My successful clients are able to move forward and live more productive and happier lives.


I believe that counseling is a collaborative endeavor which takes place in a supportive setting. Together, we can work to address issues, learn strategies and develop coping skills.
