Edie Kleinman

Licensed Professional Counselor, LCPC, NCC

I warmly welcome you to this site. My name is Edie and I have provided professional counseling services to Chicago and the northern suburbs for over 20 years. My clients can rely on my strict confidentiality, willingness to accomodate work schedules and my quick response to inquiries. Please take a moment to look at my services to find the right fit for your needs.

My Services

I have experience with couples and individuals going through life and communication issues. I have worked with many individuals who have lost a spouse or partner through death or divorce and need help and support coping.

I also work with adults coping with career transitions or anger management issues.

One of my strengths is that I provide a good listening ear, give suggestions if the client is open to it and work to help the clients reach their potential.

My ideal client is one committed to reaching treatment goals, following up with appointments, homework and beginning to take positive steps

All are welcome

Clients are unique and all are welcome. I am here to help individuals and couples of all faiths.

Grief Counseling

Having been in “those shoes”, I am uniquely qualified to help individuals with personal grief issues.

Life Transitions

Clients who are experiencing life transitions are welcome.

Court Referals

I work with local courts to help individuals with anger management and parenting issues.

I also work with EAP (Employment Assistance Program) referrals.
